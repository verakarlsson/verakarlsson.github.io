Postproductions at studiengalerie-1357 2023 The Institute for Scene Experiments was an ongoing project exploring the properties, limits, and possibilities of scenes in film and cinema, my participation started as actress and collaborator in a workshop at KW in Berlin in 2022. At studiegalere-1357 I presented work in from of a video edit from the workshop and a collage work in form of a pamphlet with doodles, text, electrical tape and images, 2023.
