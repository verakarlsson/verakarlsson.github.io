Fermata, (2021) a collaboration between Vera Karlsson and Iman Mohammed. The video is a result from our meeting at Gotland film institute for a four days during Fermata, a collaboration between Vera Karlsson and Swedish poet Iman Mohammed. The video is a result from our meeting at Gotland film institute for a four days during summer 2021. The work documents Iman's memories and relationship to the iland and specifically the peninsula Fårö. Gotland is also the place where I grew up and our friendship was formed later in Stockholm. The work discuss the implications of belonging to a place and what that means. It is a portrait of Mohammed, and includes cinema and music that inspires her work as a poet, Lost Highway and Cuadecuc, vampir, are quoted in the film. The work also has an undertone of Ingmar Bergmans film Persona. The title comes from Iman's second poetry collection "Fermata" (2018) which she reads from. I filmed, edited and made the score for the work.
