Amputated formats, 2023, presented at Compound, Systema, Marsielles 2023
Collage book with photos from fire-exit staircase of same housing building as my works in Keywork and Keyworks (2022) where shot, arragned with a fractured 'cut-up' narrative regarding the potential of friendship. The work is a standalone sequel to the works presented in Keywork and Keyworks. It continues the question of fragmentation as a critic of the idea of a whole and as a model for asset making.
